Streams Of Your Interest

CBSE offers 3 stream options which determine the subjects you will study in your class 11th and 12th and the career options you would ultimately have in the future.

Science

Science is both medical or non-medical stream. It is a popular choice of subject among students especially those who wish to be a doctor or a engineer. Science is a multidisciplined field with new disciplines and branches being added with the advancement of science and technology.

Commerce

Commerce is a field that deals with accountancy, money, monetary, source, investiments, etc. A Chartered Accountant is one of the best career options for commerce students. You can have a wonderful 'comfortable' career in this stream.

Arts/Humanities

Education in humanities or liberal arts offers a number of career options. It include the study of ancient and modern languages, literature, philosophy, history, archaeology, anthropology, human geography, law, politics, religion, and art.
